Our Goal

At toThePoint Tutoring, our ultimate mission is to empower students with the knowledge and skills necessary to excel academically and beyond. With a focus on personalized learning, we tailor our tutoring services to cater to the unique learning styles of each student. Through a question-oriented approach, we encourage active engagement, rather than simply providing answers.

Our team is purposefully composed of recent and current students, ensuring they can best understand the experiences and challenges students face. Our highly qualified and passionate tutors are committed to fostering a supportive environment, where students feel encouraged to ask questions, explore new concepts, and build their confidence. At toThePoint Tutoring, we strive to cultivate a love for learning and enable students to embrace challenges with confidence and curiosity.

General Information

Students have the choice to meet with our tutors either online or in person at the Westport Library. We highly recommend our in-person sessions at the Westport Library, as they provide an engaging learning environment where students can focus.

We offer our tutoring services at a rate of $50 per hour, with a minimum session duration of one hour and a maximum of two hours. Our tutors are committed to providing personalized support to help students achieve their academic goals, and they aim to be as flexible as possible with scheduling a time that works for students. Once you have found a tutor through our “Schedule a Session” page, copy the listed email and send a detailed email.
